# Creation

A guide to creative spaces, resources, and opportunities for making art, music, crafts, and more in Omaha and nearby.

## What you'll find here
- Studios, workshops, and maker spaces
- Art supply stores and creative retailers
- Community art programs and classes
- Opportunities for collaboration and exhibition
- Local creative organizations

## Featured Creative Spaces

- [Metro Community College North Campus Fabrication Lab](https://www.mccneb.edu/Community-Business/Workforce-Innovation-Division/Fabrication-Lab) — Maker space with 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC, woodworking, metalworking, electronics, and more. Open to students and the public for classes, workshops, and open lab hours. Located at MCC North Express Center, 104th & Fort St.

- [Metro West Omaha Campus Pottery Class](https://www.mccneb.edu/Prospective-Students/Programs-of-Study/Art) — Hands-on pottery and ceramics classes for all skill levels, offered through MCC's West Omaha campus. Learn wheel throwing, hand-building, glazing, and more. Open to students and community members. Located at 204th & West Dodge Rd.

- [DoSpace Omaha](https://www.dospace.org/) — Free, public technology and innovation center with 3D printers, laser cutters, computers, software, and a variety of creative programs for all ages. Offers maker space access, workshops, and community events. Located at 72nd & Dodge.

- [Third Space](https://www.thirdspaceomaha.org/events) — Community-driven creative space hosting art shows, workshops, performances, and events for all ages. Focused on inclusivity, collaboration, and supporting local artists. Check their calendar for upcoming happenings.

## Grants & Funding

- [Amplify Arts Grants](https://www.amplifyarts.org/) — Omaha-based nonprofit supporting artists and creatives through grants, fellowships, and project funding. Open calls for local and regional artists, with a focus on equity and community impact. Check their site for current opportunities and deadlines.
	- [Alternate Currents](https://www.amplifyarts.org/alternate-currents) — Artist-driven regranting program supporting experimental, collaborative, and community-based projects in Omaha and the region.
