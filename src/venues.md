## Downtown

### The Verdict
Trans/nonbinary contingent. Gendered bathrooms, several stalls in each.

Weekly events ran by similar but distinct people. Goth Night is the longest-running, Industrial is more my jam.

- **New Wave Night**: 1st Saturday, put on your ripped stockings
- **The Slab**: 2nd Sat, put on your battle jacket
- **Goth Night ([Club Noir](https://www.eventbrite.com/o/noir-omaha-59519005883))**: 3rd Sat, gel your hair and danse macabre
- **Industrial Night ([Club Sinthesis](https://www.facebook.com/clubsinthesis/))**: Last Sat, shave your head and put on your gear

<a id="benson"></a>
## Benson

### Shakedown Street
Dive bar next to Petshop with drag / burlesque shows and music. Modular Synthesizer Group meets there 8-10 on the last Wed of the month (typically). Bathrooms are single-stall. Punk/queer space.

[Website](https://www.facebook.com/shakedownstreetomaha/)

---

### The Waiting Room
Large venue for touring acts, local shows, and dance parties. Hosts a wide range of genres and events. All-ages shows are common. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://www.waitingroomlounge.com/)

---

### Reverb Lounge
Smaller venue next to The Waiting Room. Hosts indie, punk, electronic, and local acts. Intimate space with a bar and patio. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://www.reverblounge.com/)

## Midtown

### O'Leaver's
Venue for small/medium-sized acts. Punk/indie/queer vibe. Legendarily gross bathrooms.

[Website](https://www.oleavers.com/)

---


### Joslyn Castle Carriage House
Historic venue for arts, music, and community events. Hosts concerts, performances, workshops, and private gatherings in a unique castle setting. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://joslyncastle.com/)
