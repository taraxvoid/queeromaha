## O'Leaver's

Venue for small/medium-sized acts. Punk/indie/queer vibe. Legendarily gross bathrooms.

[Website](https://www.oleavers.com/)

---

## The Verdict (Downtown)

Trans/nonbinary contingent at most events. Gendered bathrooms, several stalls in each.

- **New Wave Night**: Dance party dedicated to classic and modern new wave hits. 1st Saturday
- **The Slab**: 2nd Sat
- **Goth Night Club Noir**: 3rd Sat
- **Club Sinthesis**: Last Sat

[Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/verdictbaromaha/)

[Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/verdictbaromaha)

[Website](https://www.theverdictomaha.com/events.html)

---

## Petshop

Punk/queer space for provocative art and experimental music. Only open to the public for BFF nights and shows/events. Bathrooms are single-stall. PetFest is once/year and it's rad

[Website](https://www.bffomaha.org/petshop)

---

## Shakedown Street (Benson)

Right next to Petshop, a dive bar that has drag / burlesque shows and music. The Modular Synth group meets there on the last Wed of the month (typically). Bathrooms are single-stall.

[Website](https://www.facebook.com/shakedownstreetomaha/)

---

## Low End at Bemis (Downtown)

Concert series and venue for experimental, electronic, and adventurous music at the Bemis Center for Contemporary Arts. Features local, national, and international artists.

[Website](https://www.bemiscenter.org/programs/low-end)

---

## Flixx Lounge (Downtown)

Weekly Drag shows. Notably, Flixx is much more queer than the larger bar down the street (which shall not be named).

[Website](https://www.flixxlounge.com/)

---

## The Waiting Room (Benson)

Large venue for touring acts, local shows, and dance parties. Hosts a wide range of genres and events. All-ages shows are common. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://www.waitingroomlounge.com/)

---

## Reverb Lounge (Benson)

Smaller venue next to The Waiting Room. Hosts indie, punk, electronic, and local acts. Intimate space with a bar and patio. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://www.reverblounge.com/)

---

## Joslyn Castle Carriage House (Omaha)

Historic venue for arts, music, and community events. Hosts concerts, performances, workshops, and private gatherings in a unique castle setting. Bathrooms are multi-stall, gendered.

[Website](https://joslyncastle.com/)
