# Queer-Supportive Cafes

- 🚻 = Single-stall BR

## Mill on Leavenworth (Midtown)

Large cafe, seating is limited during busy times. Gendered bathrooms with stalls.
[Website](https://millcoffee.com/)

---

## Roast Coffeehouse (Aksarben Village + 114th & Blondo) 🚻

Great coffee and pastries, supportive staff and owners.
[Website](https://www.roastcoffeehouse.com/)

---

## Urban Abbey (Old Market)

Operated by progressive United Methodists. Has bookstore with a lot of queer literature and varied traditions. Bathrooms are "separate" but all-gender. [Website](https://www.urbanabbeyomaha.com/)

---

## Edge of the Universe Cafe (Benson) 🚻

The owners are artists who decorate with themed art. Good to chill there after BFF or have an intimate date. Some padded chairs, booths are uncomfortable. [Website](https://www.edgeoftheuniverseomaha.com/)

---

## Legends Comics & Coffee (Benson) 🚻

Comic shop with a queer section. Has meetups and a board game backroom. Seating is OK. [FB](https://www.facebook.com/LegendComics/) [IG](https://www.instagram.com/legendcomicsandcoffee/?hl=en)

---


## Spielbound (Midtown) 🚻

Board game cafe with lots of meetups. Good place for a date / group. Seating is pretty uncomfortable. The main bathrooms are gendered.

[Website](https://www.spielbound.com/how-to-spielbound) [Active Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/playing-games-at-spielbound/)

---


## Blue Line Coffee (Dundee) 🚻

Good coffee and pastries, cool staff. Has flyers for local shows and events. Not a lot of seating. Art books and magazines to read. [FB](https://www.facebook.com/pages/Blue-Line-Coffee/140611479308727) [IG](https://www.instagram.com/bluelinecoffeeomaha/?hl=en)

---


## Archetype Coffee (Blackstone & Little Bohemia) 🚻

The one, the only. Seating is pretty uncomfortable. [Website](https://archetypecoffee.com/)

---

## Sweet Magnolias (Midtown)

Pastry shop, no coffee T_T [Website](https://www.sweetmagnoliasbakeshop.com//)

---

## Reading Room Omaha (Downtown)

Bookstore cafe thing. Has a poetry open mic night. [Website](https://readingroomomaha.com/events/)
